**Conductor** is an agentic AI assistant that lets instructors manage Canvas courses through natural language. Libretto documents how that product was conceived, designed, and built.

The mission and vision live in the project charter on the `Documents` branch of the capstone repository.

<Evidence id="EV-001" />

The system runs as a microservice stack behind Kong, with a React frontend and dedicated auth, canvas, agentic, and notification services.
